About Japan Student Living

12 years of excellence in student accommodation

Our Story

Since 2013, we have been dedicated to providing premium, fully furnished apartments to international students in Japan. Our mission is to make your transition to studying in Tokyo as smooth and comfortable as possible.

With over 800 students in our community and 12 years of experience, we understand the unique needs of international students. From prime locations near universities to 24/7 multilingual support, we're here to ensure your Japanese adventure starts on the right foot.

Our Journey

2013

Foundation

Japan Student Living was founded with a vision to revolutionize student accommodation in Tokyo.

2016

Expansion

Expanded to serve students in Shibuya, Shinjuku, and Harajuku districts.

2019

Community Growth

Reached 500+ international students from 25+ countries.

2025

Today

Proudly serving 800+ students from 35+ countries with 150+ properties.
